Welcome to Windsurfer

Log your training sessions so that you can keep track of your progress.
Include details about the wind conditions, how you felt on the water during your training session and what you were working on.
You can inlcude comments from your coach or what you want to work on in your next session in the details box too.
There's a limit of one log per specified date so if you had multiple training sessions on one day, include them all in one log.
There is a time-on-the-water limit of 6 hours...remember not to over do it, rest is an important part of training too!
We hope you enjoy this tool and find it useful in your windsurfing training journey!
